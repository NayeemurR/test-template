About Erazer

Personal Data Removal with Erazer

At Erazer, we make tools to help protect your personal data on the internet. Our first offerings include removing your information from data brokers, removing your images from unwanted sites, and scanning the dark web for any leaked personal data. We're building your own personal cybersecurity guard, an all in one product that can protect all aspects of your vibrant and complicated digital life. We use advanced technologies like AI to help keep your data safe and remove it efficiently from the internet. Erazer makes it super easy to remove your data and make sure you're protected from cybercrime and or other threats at all times.

  • Automated data scanning & removal
  • Dark web monitoring
  • 24/7 customer support
  • Image takedowns
